Programs

Virtual programming is available, see our program calendar. Check at the MH Library for weekly take-and-make crafts.

Volunteering

Teens and adults can become library volunteers for shelving and homework assistance by picking up an application at the reference desk, with a minimum age requirement of 14 and a commitment of at least two hours per week for three months.

After-Hours Bookdrop

On the parking lot/plaza side of the library there is an outside bookdrop for after-hours returns.

Library Study Rooms

Discover four study rooms at Mountain House Library: three small rooms accommodating up to 6 people each, and a larger conference-sized room for up to 12, available for advance reservations in person or by phone, with rules outlined for usage.
